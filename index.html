Life in Lockdown - 2020

I was the front-end developer on a 24hr hackathon collaboration project using Unreal Engine 4. Life in Lockdown gives the player freedom and a method to express their frustrations while maintaining a holistic view of the problems people generally face in quarantine. To survive the game the player completes tasks to maintain their finances, hunger, social health, and physical health all while avoiding the enemy (Covid-19).

 Unreal Engine 4

Furmilliar - 2020

I Designed a low-fidelity mobile application using Marvel. Furmilliar is an app which allows animal lovers to collect data about ferral cats. The data collected is used by scientists to support ferral cat research.
